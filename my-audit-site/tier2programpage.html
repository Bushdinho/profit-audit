Via WhatsApp Group call or Zoom — we confirm the format with each cohort. Calls are 60–90 minutes, bi-weekly, and recordings are shared after each session for those who can't attend live.

Tier 2 is a group coaching program with 7 new modules and 6 live calls. Tier 3 is for operators ready to scale to the next level with hands-on support.
