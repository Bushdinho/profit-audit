Questions

Before You Apply

Do I need to complete Tier 1 and Tier 2 first?+
Tier 1 is required. Tier 2 is strongly recommended. The Tier 3 partnership assumes your import infrastructure (sourcing, payments, shipping) is working. We'll review your Tier 1 completion during the application conversation.
Why is it limited to 5 clients?+
The 1-on-1 monthly sessions and the level of attention this program requires cannot be maintained with more clients. 5 allows genuine depth of work with each client. When a cohort is full, you join a waitlist for the next one.
Can I pay in instalments?+
Yes. We typically structure Tier 3 as: GH₵ 8,000 at enrolment, then GH₵ 4,000/month for months 2–5. This aligns your payments with the value you're receiving each month. Discuss the specific structure during your application call.
What does "done-with-you" mean in practice?+
Done-with-you means we work alongside you — not instead of you. You still run your business. We review your numbers, identify what to do next, and give you the exact actions each month. The personal introductions to US/UK partners are done for you. The strategy and implementation is done with you.
How is this different from just hiring a consultant?+
A consultant tells you what to do and bills by the hour. This is a structured partnership with a specific outcome guarantee (40% revenue growth), a proven curriculum, peer access (cohort co-buys), and a founder who is actively running the same type of business right now. You're learning from current operational experience, not historical advice.
